Consultant agrees to provide support for the upcoming Heliospheric Medium-Class Explorers (MIDEX) proposal cycle, highlighting the requirement for supplementary Time History of Events and Macroscale Interactions During Substorms (THEMIS- NASA mission) information to strengthen the expected Code 670 submission. The supplier is tasked with generating a database or Excel spreadsheet that includes the THEMIS A-E bow shock crossings for the year 2007, featuring Geocentric Solar Ecliptic/Geocentric Solar Magnetospheric (GSE/GSM -coordinate systems)  locations, ion density, velocity, temperature, magnetic field strength and direction on both sides of the boundary, along with the prevailing time-lagged NASA Database (OMNI) solar wind plasma and magnetic field conditions.
